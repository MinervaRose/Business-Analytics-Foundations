This repository preserves a selection of projects completed during the Bertelsmann Data Science Scholarship and subsequent analytics training pathway.

While these projects predate my later work in artificial intelligence and machine learning, they illustrate the foundations of a skillset that remains central to my work today:

* transforming data into actionable insights
* communicating findings to stakeholders
* designing dashboards and visualizations
* evaluating business performance
* supporting evidence-based decision making

## Included Projects

### Revenue Concentration Analysis

A business intelligence case study investigating whether revenue is concentrated among a small number of products or distributed across a broader product portfolio.

**Skills demonstrated**

* Data storytelling
* Executive communication
* Business risk analysis
* Data visualization

---

### Dashboard Design

Development of dashboard concepts and visual analytics solutions designed to communicate business performance clearly and efficiently.

**Skills demonstrated**

* Tableau
* KPI design
* Dashboard architecture
* Visual communication

---

### Crafting an Analytic Brief

Development of an analytical framework including customer segmentation, customer journey mapping, KPI definition, and experimentation planning.

**Skills demonstrated**

* Customer analytics
* Funnel analysis
* Metric selection
* Strategic planning

---

### Black Friday Campaign Performance Analysis

Evaluation of campaign objectives, audience segments, acquisition efficiency, product categories, and overall commercial performance.

**Skills demonstrated**

* Marketing analytics
* Performance reporting
* ROI evaluation
* Executive reporting
